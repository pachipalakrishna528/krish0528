About Us

Mission

Realizing the fact and importance of optimization in usage of natural resources. We at SEFE (Smart Environment Friendly Economical) Automations Private Limited out of our objectives we have started working on optimization technologies for preventing wastages in a different usage pattern. We believe this is our techno Commercial responsibility to be part of Kyoto protocol signed by almost all every country on the globe.

Technologies

The electrical industry on the globe have resolved IEC and constituted different committees, which in their series of meetings have laid guidelines and devised various universal IEC protocols for electrical industry to energy generation, transmission, distribution, regulations, usage etc. The. automation with the integration of electronics, embedded, communications, IT and big data base management. The SEFE automation systems with its objective of design and development of technologies suitable for the products used for electrical distribution of all types including new and renewable energies.

Corporate values

Realizing the fact, the SEFE automation has taken oath to develop and to do the business which will largely contribute the development of the society in all the aspects and cultivates the habit of protecting resources. The SEFE automation would like to operate in healthy global competition and serve the society to the fullest satisfaction of every section. SEFE has drawn its quality policy matching to all the standards in the globe.

Services

SEFE Automation Systems Pvt. Ltd has an existing product line and also develop new products in line with its mission of developing Energy Savings equipment. Conserve natural resources, save energy, by adopting ecofriendly devices / materials by energy savings. The main applications - initially targeted are Energy monitoring and controlling equipment suitable for: CDMS - Centralized Distribution Monitoring System.
